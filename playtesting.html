Why playtesting?

Playtesting is an important part of the game design process, giving developers the feedback we need in order to make our games fun for you.

While you do get to play the game before anyone else, be aware that you are playing an unfinished product.

Playtesting for Spacingclover

When you sign up to be a playtester, you go on a list that I choose from when running a playtest.

I am not always running playtests, but when one is going I would appreciate it if you didn't wait too long before playing.

The following things will be needed from you:

  • Record your gameplay (screen recorders like obs, bandicam)
  • Take notes of what you like, what you find frustrating or confusing, what elements didn't resonate with you
  • Thoughtfully answer the post session survey

When the playtest begins, you will receive an email containing:

  • An executable (.exe)
  • A link to a post-session survey
  • Instructions for conducting the playtest
  • A fun picture of an animal

No pressure

You don't have to playtest if you don't want. While it isn't a big time commitment, I understand that some people can't spare the time.

This is a big favor for me, and if you help me out, I will be very grateful, and the final game will be better.
